Netflix Dashboard

Click here to interact with Netflix Dashboard


Instructions for use: To effectively navigate this Dashboard, begin by selecting the desired media type and choosing a specific title. This action will unveil essential details such as Rating, Release Year, Duration, Date Added, Genre, and Description. The interactive map will additionally highlight the country of origin. To reset the Dashboard, simply choose "All" for both media type and title. Moreover, the map and rating panels serve as dynamic filters. For instance, to explore titles originating in Russia, select Russia on the map. This action will automatically adjust Ratings, Distribution, and Genres based on the selected titles. Similarly, the Ratings panel will adapt the map, distribution, and genres according to the chosen rating. This interactivity provides a seamless and tailored exploration of the content in the Dashboard.

The dashboard linked above explores Netflix TV show and Movie data. The dashboard offers an insightful and user-friendly exploration of the streaming platform's extensive content library. Users can seamlessly navigate through a curated selection of information, including the duration, title, and rating of each available movie or TV show. The dashboard not only provides a comprehensive overview but also highlights the top 10 genres, allowing users to quickly identify and explore content based on their preferences. With a clean and intuitive design, this dashboard enhances the Netflix viewing experience, making it easier for users to discover and enjoy content tailored to their interests.
