HR Dashboard

Click here to interact with HR Analytics Dashboard

Instructions for use: To navigate this dashboard, select the education field(s) you would like to view from the dropdown in the upper right-hand corner. Additionally, the 'Attrition by Gender,' 'Department-Wise Attrition,' and 'Education Field-Wise Attrition' panels serve as their own filters. If you would like to focus on female employees with a Bachelor's degree currently working in the HR department, select 'Bachelor's degree' in the upper right-hand dropdown, then choose 'female' in the 'Attrition by Gender' panel, and select the HR department in the pie chart within the 'Department-Wise Attrition' panel.

The HR Dashboard offers a concise yet comprehensive snapshot of critical workforce metrics. Key features include real-time insights into employee count, attrition rate and count, active employees, average age, and a visually intuitive job satisfaction rating heat map. Additionally, the dashboard breaks down attrition by education field and provides a detailed view of attrition rates by gender across different age groups. This user-friendly tool empowers HR professionals to make informed decisions, optimize workforce planning, and implement targeted strategies for employee retention and satisfaction.
