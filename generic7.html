King County Dashboard

Click here to interact with King County Dashboard


To navigate this dashboard, select the month you would like to focus on and then choose a day on the calendar. If you'd like to explore another day, deselect the one you are currently on and then select a different day on the calendar. For the month, navigate through the dropdown menu. Use the three sliders at your discretion; if no results are rendered, there are no houses meeting the criteria.

The linked dashboard delves into the dataset on King County house prices, employing advanced data visualization techniques. I strategically analyzed and showcased average house prices, heat maps, and distribution patterns for bedrooms, bathrooms, and overall house prices within King County. To optimize user experience, I integrated user-friendly features such as filters and interactive elements, elevating the accessibility and usability of the dashboard and facilitating a more profound comprehension of the intricate data surrounding King County house prices.
